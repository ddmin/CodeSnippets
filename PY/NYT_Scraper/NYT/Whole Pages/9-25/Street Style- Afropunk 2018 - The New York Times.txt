Title: Street Style: Afropunk 2018 - The New York Times

By Melissa Bunni Elian

Every year in cities around the world, the music festival Afropunk draws people from the African diaspora to celebrate and champion blackness in all of its expressions. The photographer Melissa Bunni Elian attended the event in Brooklyn and spoke to many of the attendees about how style informs their individuality.
